How to use?
Step 1 - Download app Just click “Download”.
Step 2 - Install app Install our app on your pc
Step 3 - Add Widgets to the special folder
Just download the widgets you like from our widgets page or from any source (we are not responsible for other sources).
Unzip the archive along the path: C:\Users\USERNAME\Documents\Widgets
Step 4 - Launch our app Launch our application from the icon called "Fancy Widgets" in the start menu.
Step 5 - Launch widget Launch the desired widget by clicking on the "Enable" button in the widget card
